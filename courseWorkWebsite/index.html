Dogs as Pets is a site centred around man's best friend - here you will find information on how dogs came to be domesticated pets, the most popular breeds of dog in the UK and even some information on laws centred around the ownership of dogs as pets.

On this site, I have divided information about dogs into multiple separate pages.

On the popular breeds page you will find a list of the 10 most popular dog breeds in the UK aswell as the number of pet dogs owned in the UK.

On the history of dogs page you will find a brief history of how dogs evolved and became domesticated pets.

Finally, on the legislation around dog ownership page you will find information on laws affecting dog ownership in the uk.
